Addressing your concerns

Frequently asked questions

How do I pay?
We support EcoCash, InnBucks, and ZimSwitch via Paynow escrow. Your payment is released only after you confirm the lesson.
Are tutors truly vetted?
Yes — every ID and academic certificate is manually verified before a tutor can teach on EduMatch.
Poor internet connection?
Our Lite Mode + SMS fallback system ensures you never miss lesson updates and can chat offline.
Can I find university tutors by course code?
Absolutely. Search by course code (e.g., CSIT 203) and get matched with tertiary specialists.
