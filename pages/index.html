Unique functional modules

Every differentiator lives in the product, not just in the pitch.

These modules turn EduMatch into a full tutoring ecosystem instead of a simple listing site.

Intelligent matching

Rule-based tutor recommendations

Students get matched according to syllabus type, level, location, and precise academic need.

  • ZIMSEC versus Cambridge separation for more accurate tutor fit.
  • Tertiary routing by module name or course code.
  • Better quality control than open-directory browsing.
Study Pods

Shared sessions for 3-5 learners

Students can learn together, reduce cost pressure, and automatically split tutoring fees.

  • Ideal for revision groups, neighborhoods, and classmates.
  • Creates a more affordable route to premium tutors.
  • Encourages social accountability and regular attendance.
Parent module

Guardian monitoring and approval

Parents or guardians can track sessions, progress, and payment release in real time.

  • Live oversight for younger learners.
  • Progress snapshots after tutoring milestones.
  • More trust between family, tutor, and platform.
Low bandwidth

SMS fallback and lite access

EduMatch is designed for rural and peri-urban use where internet quality is inconsistent.

  • Lesson reminders and updates via SMS.
  • Reduced-friction workflows for lower data usage.
  • Better continuity for learners outside major cities.
Syllabus tracker

Digitized learning progress against official requirements

Tutors and students can see how teaching maps to national and institutional syllabus goals.

  • Track what has been covered and what is still pending.
  • Useful for exam preparation and guardian reporting.
  • Keeps tutoring aligned with real outcomes.
Gamification

Streaks, badges, and retention loops

Student motivation is reinforced through visible progress markers that reward consistency.

  • Attendance streaks for session discipline.
  • Achievement badges for progress milestones.
  • Supports stronger long-term engagement.
